Ferret Master

Gain total fretboard control within years - Start now!

    Learning all kinds of arpeggios and scales can be painful.
    But do not worry - this friendly ferret is here to help.
    It will show you some playable forms.

    White dots all root notes on the whole fretboard.
    Red dots are the root note within the current form.
    Yellow dots are the other notes of the form.

    Pro tip:
    Use the q,we,r,t,y... keys to activate piano keys,
    esc. to reset the piano,
    left & right keys to skip through the results!
